:::tip[This Week's Focus]
Understanding tendency and coincidence evidence rules, their requirements, and the relationship with character evidence principles.

## 🔍 Key Legal Principles

:::caution[Tendency Evidence Requirements]
Must show significant probative value and cannot be used solely to prove bad character or propensity.
:::

:::note[Coincidence Evidence]
Focuses on improbability of events occurring by coincidence rather than tendency to act in a particular way.
:::
