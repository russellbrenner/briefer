:::tip[This Week's Focus]
Understanding credibility evidence rules, cross-examination principles, and the balance between testing witness credibility and unfair prejudice.

## 🔍 Key Legal Principles

:::caution[Credibility Evidence]
Evidence relevant only to credibility is generally inadmissible, subject to specific exceptions for cross-examination.
:::

:::note[Cross-Examination Scope]
Cross-examination may test credibility but must not stray into irrelevant or unduly prejudicial territory.
:::
